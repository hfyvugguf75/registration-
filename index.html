19-Year-Old Cyber Criminal Arrested in Quetta for Dark Web Activities

Date: December 18, 2024

Quetta: A 19-year-old boy, Harsh Sanam, known for his alleged involvement in illegal dark web activities, was arrested today in a high-profile operation conducted by law enforcement agencies. Authorities have described him as a dangerous hacker responsible for various cybercrimes.

Harsh was reportedly involved in hacking secure systems, stealing sensitive data, and conducting illicit transactions on the dark web. Sources claim he was operating as part of an underground network, exploiting vulnerabilities in financial and governmental systems.

Investigators revealed that Harsh had developed sophisticated methods to evade detection, making his arrest a significant breakthrough in combating cybercrime. Evidence collected from his devices has uncovered details of illegal activities linked to several international organizations.

Officials have urged citizens to strengthen their cybersecurity measures as incidents of hacking continue to rise. Further investigations are ongoing to determine the extent of Harsh's network and the individuals involved.
